  2. Master of Architecture – Degree class of Architecture and Construction Engineering
    University of Naples Federico II
    Naples, Italy – September 2009 to July 2015
    Final grade: 110 Con Lode / 110, and recommendation for publication
    Average grade: 29.167 / 30
    I defended the thesis “Computational morphogenesis and construction of an acoustic shell for outdoor chamber music”.
    Implementing numerical methods that uses geometrical acoustics, computational physics, descriptive geometry and genetic algorithms, the thesis presents a novel generative method to design a passive acoustic shell for outdoor classical music. Both in silico results and on site measurements show that it outperforms the state of the art of the design of outdoor acoustic chambers.
