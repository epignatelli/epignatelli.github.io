Hello there, I am Eduardo,
researcher in AI.

My research is at the intersection of Deep Reinforcement Learning and Large Language Models with a focus on the Credit Assignment Problem. I am interested in solving the fundamental problem of how Artificial Intelligence (AI) evaluates actions and how to scale RL to the real world and achieve open-ended learning agents. I currently act as a Lecturer at UCL, for the Integrated Machine Learning Systems master course. Previously, I was a PhD student at UCL with Dr Laura Toni and Dr Tim Rocktäschel in the Learning and Signal Processing (LASP) group. Before that, I was a postdoc at the Biologically-Inspired Computation and Inference Lab (BICI) at the Imperial College, and the Machine Learning Lead at BuroHappold Engineering.
